A LOFAR Transients Key Science Project meeting will be held in Amsterdam on 3–5 December 2012. This coincides with the start of LOFAR "Cycle 0"; a major theme of the meeting will therefore be to ensure we are best able to take advantage of the anticipated avalance of data. Of course, we will also consider longer term plans and ongoing development. All Transients Key Project members and invited guests are welcome.

Registration & Costs

Please register for the meeting by sending mail to meetings@transientskp.org. Include the following information:

  • Name
  • Affiliation
  • Proposed talk title (if applicable)
  • Other remarks (e.g. dietary requiremnts)

Together with your registration, please feel free to include suggestions of specific topics, or, indeed, general themes, which you think should be covered at the meeting.

Note that meeting participants are expected to make a contribution towards costs. The precise amount has yet to be determined; we anticipate that it will be around €60. This covers refreshments and lunches served during the meeting and the workshop dinner. Please be prepared to pay this in cash on arrival at the meeting.

Venue & Travel

The meeting will take place at the Astronomical Institute "Anton Pannekoek" (or "API"):

Science Park 904
1098 XH Amsterdam

The API is easily reached by public transport from the centre of Amsterdam and from Schiphol International Airport. The API website provides comprehensive travel information, but please feel free to contact the LOC if you need help finding us.

Accommodation

A block booking has been made at the Eden Hotel Amsterdam. Rooms are available at the special workshop rate of €98 per night. The Eden Hotel Amsterdam is located right in the centre of Amsterdam, about half an hour from the API by public transport. Maps and directions are availble from Google.

If you wish to take advantage of the block booking, you must book using this link. Note that the block booking expires on 5 November.

Catering

Lunches and coffee breaks will be provided every day during the meeting; refer to the schedule for details (when available).

On the evening of Tuesday 4 December we will meet for a workshop dinner. The details and venue have yet to be announced.

All of the above are included in the registration fee. Other meals or refreshments are up to you, but do feel free to ask the organisers for suggestions.
