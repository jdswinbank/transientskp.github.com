A LOFAR Transients Key Science Project meeting will be held in Amsterdam on 3–5 December 2012. This coincides with the start of LOFAR "Cycle 0"; a major theme of the meeting will therefore be to ensure we are best able to take advantage of this data. Of course, we will also consider longer term plans and ongoing development. All Transients Key Project members and invited guests are welcome.

Registration & Costs

Please register for the meeting by sending mail to meetings@transientskp.org. Include the following information:

  • Name
  • Affiliation
  • Proposed talk title (if applicable)
  • Other remarks (e.g. dietary requiremnts)

Together with your registration, please feel free to include suggestions of specific topics, or, indeed, general themes, which you think should be covered at the meeting.

Note that meeting participants are expected to make a contribution towards costs. The precise amount has yet to be determined; we anticipate that it will be around €60. This covers refreshments and lunches served during the meeting and the workshop dinner. Please be prepared to pay this in cash on arrival at the meeting.

Venue & Travel

The meeting will take place at the Astronomical Institute "Anton Pannekoek" (or "API"):

Science Park 904
1098 XH Amsterdam

The API is easily reached by public transport from the centre of Amsterdam and from Schiphol International Airport. The API website provides comprehensive travel information, but please feel free to contact the LOC if you need help finding us.

When you arrive at the API, report to the main reception desk where you will be issued with a visitor pass. Take the lift to the fourth floor. The meeting room is immediately outside the lift, and will be clearly signposted.
