Location

The Eden Hotel Amsterdam is the perfect hotel to start exploring Amsterdam. The hotel is located in the vibrant heart of the Dutch capital, next to the famous Rembrandt square. As the hotel is situated on the banks of the Amstel river, several rooms have breathtaking views on the typical Dutch lift-up bridges and passing boats. Shops, cafes, bars and clubs are in the direct vicinity of the hotel. The Eden Hotel Amsterdam can easily be reached by public transportation.
