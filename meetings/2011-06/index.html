Welcome

The LOFAR Transients Key Science Project collaboration meeting will be held in Amsterdam on June 27—29 2011. All Transients Project members, and their invited guests, are welcome: please register with the organisers if you haven't yet done so.

We hope to make the meeting informal, like the previous ones, and keep it cheap. We shall collect some money during the meeting for lunches and a dinner, but otherwise there will be no fees. The meeting will be in the new Faculty of Science building, Science Park 904, Amsterdam (directions are available here). We hope to welcome many of you to Amsterdam!

We will use the EVO collaboration tool to allow collaboration members who are unable to attend in person to join the meeting virtually. See here for details.
